Our Story

Started with a fire pit.
Ended up here.

Ember Kitchen opened in 2019 with a simple idea: cook everything over live fire, use what's in season, and let the flavors do the work. No foam, no tweezers, no theater.

Chef Dana Reyes trained in New Orleans and spent five years cooking barbecue across the South before landing in Nashville. The menu changes four times a year. The wood-burning hearth never stops.
