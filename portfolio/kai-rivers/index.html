About

Built for the
long listen.

Kai Rivers started writing music in a Portland basement with a laptop and too much time. Eight years later, the laptop is better and the basement is rented.

Four studio albums, a handful of EPs, and a touring circuit that's taken the project from 200-person venues to festival mainstages. The sound has always been the same: ambient textures with enough rhythm to keep you moving, lyrics that earn the second listen.
